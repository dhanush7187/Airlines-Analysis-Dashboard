# ✈️ Airline Analysis Dashboard – Sky Metrics

## 📊 Overview  
**Sky Metrics** is an interactive airline analytics dashboard built using Power BI to analyze key aspects of the aviation industry, including financial performance, airline efficiency, and passenger demographics. The dashboard provides actionable insights into delays, cancellations, profitability, customer behavior, and operational trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Analyze airline performance and operational efficiency  
- Identify the most profitable routes and airlines  
- Evaluate delay patterns and cancellation trends  
- Understand passenger demographics and preferences  
- Track revenue, cost, and profit metrics over time  

---

## 📁 Dataset  
The dataset includes airline operational and financial data such as:  
- Flight schedules (departure & arrival times)  
- Delay and cancellation information  
- Ticket pricing (Economy & Business class)  
- Passenger demographics (age, gender)  
- Revenue, cost, and profit metrics  

---

## 📌 Key Features  

### 🔹 Financial Analysis  
- Total Revenue & Profit (YoY trends)  
- Most Profitable Route  
- Airline-wise expenditure and operating costs  
- Revenue comparison by quarter  

### 🔹 Airline Performance  
- On-Time Performance %  
- Cancellation Rate & Total Cancelled Flights  
- Departure & Arrival Delay Analysis  
- Top performing airlines  

### 🔹 Passenger Demographics  
- Gender distribution of passengers  
- Preferred airlines  
- Average age of flyers  
- Total passenger insights  

---

## 📈 Insights Derived  
- The most profitable route is **New Delhi → Cochin**, driving a major share of overall revenue.  
- Overall **On-Time Performance is ~68.56%**, indicating scope for operational improvement.  
- The **flight cancellation rate is ~5.19%**, with over **1,100+ cancelled flights**.  
- **Arrival delays (28.91%) are higher than departure delays (24.10%)**, showing delay propagation across flights.  
- Strong financial growth observed with **~50.65% YoY revenue increase** and **~54.63% YoY profit growth**.
